DR BILAL MALIK

Dr Bilal Malik

Aesthetic Clinician/ A&E Specialist Registrar

Dr Malik is an A&E Specialist Registrar, with an Internationally Certified Trainer background who is currently training delegates around the world. His background spans from working in numerous NHS and private hospitals providing countless treatments. He has been involved in practicing and training aesthetics extensively for a number of years. His vision is simple, to bring high quality treatments to the aesthetics world which are not just backed up by qualifications, but also, the expertise of a Doctor.

Dr Malik has vast experience in rectifying procedures performed by unqualified individuals with no medical knowledge which resulted in various complications such as infections, deformities and necrosis. Ultimately, giving the patient an overall undesirable and unsatisfactory appearance, which was the complete opposite effect of what the patient actually wanted. Having the right medical experience allows the patient’s medical history to be taken into account to ensure there are no contraindications which may be harmful to the patient.

Confidence in Dr Malik’s Clients is knowing that they are putting their trust in someone who is not only medically trained but also has the right experience and the necessary skills.

His interest in Dermatology and minor surgical procedures is what inspired him to start up an exclusive aesthetics brand (Sculpted by Doctors) which primarily focuses on safety and competency over quick fixes.

While Dr Malik started his journey solely, in a short period of time he has grown his brand which now includes highly experienced doctors, dentists, and nurses who not only have extensive portfolios in this industry, but also share the same vision as him.
