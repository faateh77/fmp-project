DR. AAMIR ZUBAIRY

Dr. aamir zubairy

Consultant Orthopedic Surgeon

Mr Aamir Zubairy qualified in 1988 from University of Karachi. He had his internship and basic surgical training in the Republic of Ireland. He completed surgical fellowship (FRCS) in 1994. He was awarded Fellowship of The Royal College of Surgeon of England in 2015. (FRCSE)

His Orthopaedic higher surgical training was in the Mersey region (Liverpool) at SpR grade for 6 years leading to CCST (Certificate of completion of Specialist Training). He completed FRCS Ed (Tr & Orth) in 2001. He then did Fellowship training in foot and ankle surgery at the Wirral Hospitals in Liverpool. He was awarded an AO trauma Fellowship in the USA with Prof James Hughes.

Mr Zubairy was awarded BOFAS travelling fellowship to USA and to Belgium. He has been trained on ankle & foot arthroscopic techniques in Amsterdam.

He completed specialist orthopaedic training in 2002 and was appointed as NHS consultant at the East Lancashire NHS Trust where he developed his practice as an orthopaedic surgeon with special interest in lower limb, adult foot and ankle disorders. He now leads a multidisciplinary team of healthcare professionals providing care for routine and complex foot and ankle disorders along with trauma and general orthopaedics. He teaches on several national courses and has developed a multidisciplinary course locally that has been running very successfully for the past 5 years.

His research interests include post operative pain relief, ankle fractures and arthritis, 1st MTP joint replacement, informed consent, ankle fractures, musculoskeletal ultrasound and outcome measures. His work has been widely published in peer reviewed journals and has been presented world wide in international meetings. He has RCT studies in progress and he also contributes to NIHR portfolio studies.

His clinical interests are minimally invasive surgery (MIS), arthroscopic foot & ankle surgery, total ankle replacement and Extra Corporeal Shock Wave Treatment (ESWT) for tendinopathies.

He is education lead for the department of trauma & orthopaedics. He is educational supervisor and trainer for;

  • Medical students for SSM and Project Options University examiner for OSCE
  • Foundation year (FY I & II) doctors including patient safety lessons learnt facilitator
  • Core trainees (CT)
  • Speciality trainee registrars and fellows in foot & ankle surgery (ST)
  • Senior clinical lecturer (H) University of Central Lancashire and Lancaster
  • MRCS and FRCS (Tr & Orth) examiner

Medicolegal Practice: Mr Zubairy’s practice includes medicolegal reporting for personal injury and clinical negligence cases.

Aesthetics: Mr Zubairy is trained in non surgical aesthetic procedures including facial botox, HA facial and lip fillers, hyperhidrosis and others.
